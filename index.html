Who We Are

Local experts you can rely on

Ac-Dc Security Systems is a professional security and technology company based in Tympaki, Heraklion, Crete. We specialise in designing, installing, and maintaining complete security solutions for residential and commercial clients.

With years of hands-on experience across Crete, we combine technical expertise with a genuine commitment to quality. Every system we install is tailored to the specific needs of the site — no off-the-shelf solutions, no shortcuts.

From a single camera to a full building fit-out, we handle the entire project ourselves — from initial assessment through to long-term support.
